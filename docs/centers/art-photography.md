## Istana — Singapore

The Istana estate in Singapore is the official residence of the President. Located in the Orchard Road area, it includes the Istana Main Building and expansive gardens. The estate hosts state functions and is open to the public on special occasions, providing a glimpse into the President's residence and serving as a symbol of national pride.

---

## Nan Lian Garden — Diamond Hill, Hong Kong

Nestled in Diamond Hill, the Nan Lian Garden is a serene sanctuary where man and nature harmonise. Delicate pathways wind through lush foliage, leading to a shimmering lotus pond and enchanting pavilions. With its meticulously curated landscape and traditional Chinese design, the garden embodies tranquility and offers a respite from the outside world.

---

## Cherry Blossom at Shukugawa Park — Nishinomiya City, Japan

Shukugawa Park in Nishinomiya City, Japan, is famous for its cherry blossoms. During late March to early April, the park becomes a stunning sight with pink and white flowers. People come to have picnics, walk around, and enjoy the beautiful scenery.

---

## Flying Kite in Sai Kung — Hong Kong

This colourful kite creates a pleasant contrast against the beautiful blue sky. On weekends, the Sai Kung waterfront becomes bustling with adorable dogs and people. You will come across vendors selling kites, pet accessories, seafood, and many other fun things.
