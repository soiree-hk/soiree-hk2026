## China Express, Take Away and Delivery — Osaka, Japan

In Osaka, there are so many food outlets, but China Express is located in a rather odd place, along a busy road with only a few buildings nearby. The bright yellow tent-like rain shade makes the whole building look unique. It is a piece of history as the catering place is now closed, but it brought a lot of warmth and friendship to many residents in Osaka by offering not only delicious food but also love over the past 20 years.

---

## Café Lodge — Japan

Located in a peaceful suburb of Japan, this charming café stands out with its Western-style architecture. Amidst the sea of grey rooftops, its bright orange-yellow colour brings a cheerful atmosphere to the entire community. The interior is decorated in a sophisticated English style — each piece of decoration adds to the ambiance, making it feel like a museum.

---

## The Wave in Singapore

The Wave is an indoor sports hall in Singapore. It is a wave-shaped structure that combines nature and technology. The wave's surface sparkles with colours and reflects light, creating a beautiful sight. It represents unity, creativity, and the endless potential of human imagination.

---

## The Shaw Auditorium — Clearwater Bay, Hong Kong

The Shaw Auditorium is a round-shaped building located in Clearwater Bay, Hong Kong. It serves as a multi-purpose space, functioning as a concert hall, study area, and meeting place. The auditorium hosts various cultural and artistic activities, while also providing a quiet space for students to focus on their studies.
