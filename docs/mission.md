# Mission & Vision

SOIRÉE's mission is to promote the interaction and advancement of science and technology worldwide. Our objectives are as follows:

1. To provide a platform for communication, cooperation and construction in interdisciplinary science and technology.

2. To promote and organise activities for science and technology interactions among its members, as well as between scientific organisations, universities, institutions and government agencies.

3. To facilitate the exchange and collaboration in scientific research, education and business development.

4. To provide professional consultation in science and technology, as well as assistance in fostering strategic partnerships between the relevant worldwide government bodies.

---

## How We Accomplish Our Mission

To accomplish our mission, SOIRÉE will organize trade missions, forums, exhibitions and industrial visits. SOIRÉE will publicise major scientific developments and announcements of major scientific events and forums. With the strong academic credentials of its members, SOIRÉE can help our members promote their research results and assist in fostering partnerships to bridge the gap between research and commercialisation.

With a group of scientists and professionals on its executive committee, SOIRÉE will provide professional advice and consultation on scientific research, commercialisation and project management. SOIRÉE is able to assist our members to fulfil their potentials through organising training sessions, industrial attachments and internship programmes.
