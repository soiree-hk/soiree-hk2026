# Membership

SOIRÉE welcomes professionals, scholars, researchers, and practitioners who share our commitment to interdisciplinary science, technology, and collaboration across continents.

---

## Why Join SOIRÉE?

- Stay updated on major developments in culture, science and technology
- Access announcements of upcoming conferences and events
- Promote your research results through our platform
- Foster partnerships with fellow members across disciplines and continents
- Participate in forums, workshops, training sessions and industrial attachments
- Benefit from professional advice and consultation from our executive committee

---

## How to Apply

Send your application to **president.soiree@gmail.com** with:

- Full Name
- Current Affiliation / Organisation
- City & Country
- Email Address
- Area of Expertise / Research Interests
- Brief statement on why you wish to join SOIRÉE

---

## Membership Categories

| Type | Description |
|---|---|
| **Regular Member** | Professionals, researchers, and academics |
| **Student Member** | Graduate and postgraduate students |
| **Institutional Member** | Universities, research institutes, and organisations |
| **Honorary Member** | By invitation — distinguished contributors |
