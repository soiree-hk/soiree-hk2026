    Center of AiForce

    AI is revolutionizing scientific research and development in academy and industry. By leveraging vast datasets and advanced algorithms, AI can uncover hidden patterns, accelerate data analysis, and generate novel hypotheses. The Center of AiForce tackles a wide range of challenging problems in chemistry, biology and technology.

    Chemistry

    AI revolutionizes drug discovery, optimizes reactions, and predicts compound properties with unprecedented precision. Through AI-driven automation and robotics, high-throughput experimentation reaches new heights of efficiency and innovation.

    Current Projects: Homogeneous Nickle Catalysis (HONICA)

    Biology

    AI accelerates genomics, precision medicine, and drug development. It empowers image analysis and pathology, unveiling intricate patterns and aiding in disease diagnosis.

    Current Projects:

    • SARS-CoV-2
    • Complex Microbiota

    Technology

    AI is making a transformative imprint on information, energy, environment and other technology sectors.

    Current Projects: DNA Storage, Advanced Materials
